Q. check if the given no. is part of the fibonacci  series.
If so, print the continuation of fibonacci upto next 5 terms.


What is a fibonacci series?
A serie with first term 1 and second term 2 which proceeds in the fashion that
the next term is the sum of previous two terms.


Ex.
INPUT:  5
OUTPUT: 5	8	13	21	44	65
LOGIC:	1,2,3,5,8.... is the fibonacci and 5 is a member
	5+3=8, 8+5=13... so on
